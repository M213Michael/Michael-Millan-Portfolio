About

I am a mechanical engineer based in Northern California with a focus on structural design, simulation, and fabrication documentation. My background spans SolidWorks weldment and sheet metal modeling, ANSYS Static Structural FEA, and hands-on design validation through personal build projects.

At O&M Industries I produce full drawing packages for industrial fabrication clients including Tesla, Ceco Environmental, Adwest, and Nestec — primarily large-scale oxidizer systems and stainless ductwork assemblies. Outside of work I use my F-150 as an active engineering platform, taking projects from kinematic modeling through scan-to-CAD, FEA, and iterative redesign.

I am open to relocating to California, Nevada, or Arizona for a design or analysis-driven role in aerospace, defense, automotive, or off-road.
